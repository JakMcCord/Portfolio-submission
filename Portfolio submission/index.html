Who are we?

Skatebox is an independent Inline and Rollerskate store based in Scotland, with an aim to provide top quality customer care and to aid in the growth of the local skating scenes.

We aim to stock a large selection of Skates from the brands you know and love, as well as offer online fitting services so that you can feel confident that you are buying the skate thats right for you.
